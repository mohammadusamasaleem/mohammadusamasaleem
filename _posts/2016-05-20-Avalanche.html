I even did the sprites, although my ability to draw sprites is relatively mediocre. --- Avalanche is a top-scrolling iPhone game. You play as a marshmallow and try to avoid the rising lava by climbing on the blocks falling from the ceiling. You have to avoid getting crushed by a falling block as well. The Github repo for the project is here: {% include icon-github.svg %}Avalanche. This project is in-progress; I expect to be finished by the end of August 2016.

The marshmallow you play as is featured both in the footer of my webpage, and the homepage.

The game is developed in SpriteKit. The phyiscs is handled by SKPhysics, and the explosions are created using SKEmitterNodes. The sprites are all drawn in Adobe Illustrator.
